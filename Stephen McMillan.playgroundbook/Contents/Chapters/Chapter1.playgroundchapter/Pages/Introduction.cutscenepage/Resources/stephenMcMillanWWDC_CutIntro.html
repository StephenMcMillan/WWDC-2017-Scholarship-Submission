Currently in my Design Technology class we are studying electronic circuits. You can see one of the timer circuits that I have created on the left. That is where I got my inspiration for this playground. I thought it would be cool to simulate a real life circuit in code. Through creating this playground I not only got to continue learning new things about my passion - coding - but I was actually able to revise the details of electronics for my upcoming exam. 😜
In short, my playground is an interactive simulation of a simple circuit board containing a battery, a switch, an LED and a resistor. The playground models a real world circuit and features beautiful animations and effects when drawing and operating the various electronic components.
